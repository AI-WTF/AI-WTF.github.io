About Me

I wouldn't say I'm the smartest or the most talented. But I have an unquenchable thirst for knowledge and use my curiosity to explore new problem spaces in hopes of contributing to the advancement and adoption of generative AI. I'm self taught and have a pretty decent grasp of LLMs capabilities and limitations. My main programming language of choice is python, but I am always looking to improve and add to my toolbelt. One language I am currently adding to my repetoire is Haskell. I know it is a daunting undertaking, but I believe the functional programming paradigms in combination with the principles of category theory could make for some interesting primitives that may enhance LLM's ability to reason and search more efficiently over complex data. This is sorta my little corner of the internet to experiment, share insights, and discuss topics I find interesting from my own research.
